Advantage of Time-Frequency Analysis

One advantage of time frequency analysis is it providing a lot more information about the audio than waveform analysis. With waveform analysis, only the amplitude over time is present, but time-frequency analysis also shows the frequency, and what frequencies are present at what point in the audio.
