Week 9

This week I downloaded three more tracks from Archive.org. I then loaded them into Sonic Visualiser, extracted spectrograms, MFCCs and Chromagrams for each of them. I exported these as CSV files. I used the CSV files to generate histograms for the MFCCs and Chromagrams.

Comparison

There is not much difference between the histograms for the three tracks. The House of the Rising Sun was an older recording and therefore lacked low end compared to the other two tracks. Every Rose Has Its Thorn seems to have a larger first histogram, which makes sense given that it is a metal song.
